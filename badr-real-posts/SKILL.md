# Badr Shaqer - Real Posts Skill (v2, slide-trained)

## Voice fingerprint

Badr writes like a senior marketing strategist who code-switches mid-sentence between Modern Standard Arabic (for frameworks and frameworks-quotes) and Khaliji vernacular (for the gut-punch lines). His posts are NOT motivational - they are diagnostic. He opens by naming a specific failure mode the reader is silently living inside ("توقف عند 30 ألف $ شهرياً"، "مشغـول لذلك أنت فقيـر"، "كذبة روتين المليونير")، then walks the reader through a named framework (RACI, 4Ds, Flywheel, LTV/CAC/PBP, Decoy Effect, Razor Strategy, Don't Eat Alone) using real-world celebrity case studies (Bezos, Musk, Buffett, Jensen Huang, Seth Godin, Jack Ma, Steve Jobs, Walter White, DiCaprio, Ronaldo, Joaquin Phoenix, Saul Goodman, Jason Statham, Grant Cardone, Penn Badgley, Paul Newman). The tone alternates between brutal ("هالبوست؟ مو معلومة. مو تحفيز. هو إنذار ناعم") and disarming-intimate ("خليني اعطيك مثال"، "حسيت لمسك"، "طحت عليه بالصدفة").

The voice is **operator-confident, never guru**. He never says "manifest" or "abundance." He says dollars-with-cents ("$21,637.00"), specific multiples ("92 مليون $")، and Khaliji idioms ("الكرف اليومي"، "طاحت فجأة"، "كرفـك"، "ينهزم"). He has a brand identity called **المبادر** (the initiator/mover) which is stamped on every cover slide. His best posts feel like a friend pulling you aside and saying "اسمع، لا تكذب على نفسك" - not a coach selling you something.

## The viral variable

After analyzing all 31 posts (range: 399 likes → 54.2K), **the single variable that separates viral from flop is hook vector**. Viral posts (DOrMyVrCChz 21.7K, DMDzaDcsfS4 23K, DSQO5wniFSM 21K) point OUTWARD at a famous brand/figure/external phenomenon the reader already has an opinion about ("هل تنجح آبل في خداعنا؟"، "لماذا أغنى رواد الأعمال لا يمتلكون 100%"، "إذا ارتديت حذاءاً ضيقا اليوم..."). Flop posts (C0yi3SFKSsk 399 likes, DL0RTLTszZT 2.1K) point INWARD at the reader's own problem state ("كم دقيقة تقدر تبقى مركز"، "لماذا 80% ينسحبون")- too inward, too generic, no external anchor. **The rule: a viral Badr post takes a famous proper noun or hyper-specific number ($21,637.00 / 30,000$ / Apple / Bezos) and weaponizes it as a question that the reader didn't know they needed answered**. The "soft alarm" voice ("هالبوست؟ مو معلومة. مو تحفيز. هو إنذار ناعم") wins when paired with a proper-noun hook. Without it, the same soft alarm flops.

## Hook archetypes

### 1. The brand-callout question
**WHEN:** Tying a marketing principle to a famous brand the reader follows.
**TEMPLATE:** هل تنجح [BRAND/PERSON] في [ACTION]؟ / لماذا [BRAND] [SURPRISING ACTION]؟
**EXAMPLES (verbatim):**
- «هل تنجح آبل في خداعنا؟»
- «لماذا خسرت كامالا الانتخابات (بنظرة تسويقية)»
- «لماذا أغنى رواد الأعمال لا يمتلكون 100% من شركــــاتهم؟»

### 2. The paradox / counter-intuition
**WHEN:** Reader is doing the "obvious" thing; you reframe.
**TEMPLATE:** [POSITIVE GOAL] / [SHOCKING IMPERATIVE]
**EXAMPLES:**
- «تطمح تصير غني هالسنة؟ / وقف البيع!»
- «أنت مشغـول / لذلك أنت فقيـر»
- «ليش اجتهــــــادك يخليــك فقير؟»
- «لماذا رواد الأعمال الأثرياء يطردون أنفسهم من شركاتهم؟»

### 3. The hyper-specific dollar autopsy
**WHEN:** Personal authority post / lessons learned.
**TEMPLATE:** لماذا صرفت / $[EXACT.CENTS] / على [VICE/MISTAKE] سنة [YEAR]
**EXAMPLES:**
- «لماذا صرفت $21,637.00 على جهلي سنة 2024»
- «حققت اكثر من 92 مليون $»

### 4. The myth-buster / "lie of X"
**WHEN:** Killing a clichéd belief in the niche.
**TEMPLATE:** كذبة [POPULAR CONCEPT] / [POPULAR ADVICE] = أغبى نصيحة في البزنس
**EXAMPLES:**
- «كذبة روتيـــــن المليونير»
- «أغبى نصيحة في البزنس: لا تضع كل بيضك في سلة واحدة»
- «توقف عن دراسة السوق / دراسة السوق = 💩»

### 5. The anti-elitism "learn from X" frame
**WHEN:** Soft-selling a marketing tactic via a low-status archetype.
**TEMPLATE:** تعلم [SKILL] / من [LOW-STATUS PROFESSION]
**EXAMPLES:**
- «تعلم التسويق / من بائع السندويش»
- «تعلم التسويق / من المتسول»
- «كيف تبيع كتاجر مخـ*درات؟»

### 6. The numbered listicle with one BIG numeral
**WHEN:** Promising a finite, scannable framework.
**TEMPLATE:** [GIANT NUMBER] / اسباب/أسرار/خطوات لـ[GOAL]
**EXAMPLES:**
- «3 اسباب لتتوقف عن التسويق»
- «أسرار لاحتراف التسويق 🤫 / 7»

### 7. The specific niche-pain stat
**WHEN:** Targeting an arabic founder cohort.
**TEMPLATE:** لماذا أغلب [SEGMENT] / [SHARED PAINFUL OUTCOME]؟
**EXAMPLES:**
- «لماذا أغلب البزنسات العربية / توقف عند 30 ألف $ شهرياً؟»
- «لماذا أغلب رواد الأعمال مفلسين رغم إنهم يشتغلون 14 ساعة باليوم»
- «لماذا 80% من رواد الأعمال ينسحبون؟»

### 8. The "warning + formula" hook
**WHEN:** Promising a hidden gate the reader must pass through.
**TEMPLATE:** احذر [ACTION]! / قبل معرفة هذه [FORMULA/EQUATION]
**EXAMPLE:** «احذر توسع مشروعك! / قبل معرفة هذه المعادلة»

## Sentence rhythm rules

1. **Drop articles, break lines.** Cover slides set headline phrases on 2-3 separate lines, not a single sentence. The page break IS the punctuation.
2. **Stretch one vowel per cover.** Pick one word and elongate the middle letter for visual emphasis: «صغيييير»، «زبـــــــــائن»، «روتيـــــن»، «شركــــاتهم»، «اجتهــــــادك». Always ـــــــ, never ـ.
3. **Caption ≠ slide.** Captions are short, conversational, end with a question or a 1-line provocation. Slide headlines are bigger, sharper, with red highlights.
4. **Question-then-silence.** Caption opens with a single Khaliji question ("حصل معاك يا ترى؟ 👀"، "هل تعاني في المبيعات ؟") and ends without resolving - the resolution is "swipe."
5. **One ellipsis or em-dash per caption max.** «... لكنها الحقيقة»، «طاحت فجأة...»، «- كيف تبيع كتاجر المخد*رات -».
6. **Khaliji on the gut line, MSA on the framework line.** Frameworks ("استراتيجية الجرعة الأولى"، "تأثير الطعم") stay MSA. Emotional punches ("هالبوست"، "كرفـك"، "ينهزم"، "حسيت لمسك"، "خليني اعطيك مثال"، "طاحت") flip to Khaliji.
7. **Single emoji as punctuation.** 🤫 💩 🪣 ❤️ 👀 ☝🏻 - used as an equation operator or end-stop, never decorative. Multiple emojis only in joke contexts ("امزح امزح 😂😂").
8. **Ghost text / triple-fade for emphasis.** When a word matters, repeat it 3x with decreasing opacity ("غالي غالي غالي"، "دليلك لمضاعفة دخلك سنوياً" 3x).

## Punctuation & symbol vocabulary

- **« »** - Arabic guillemets, reserved for OLD-thinking quarantine: "« إذا ما تدخلت، الشغل يخرب »". The new thinking gets no quotes.
- **$ with cents** - never "$21K". Always "$21,637.00". The cents communicate "I tracked this."
- **Hyphen-bracketed labels** - for category labels: "- كيف تبيع كتاجر المخد*رات -".
- **🚫 NO-circle** - visual overlay on cover, means "stop doing X."
- **🤫** - secrets amplifier.
- **💩** - punchline equation operator ("X = 💩").
- **🪣** - leaky-bucket motif for retention/funnel posts.
- **☝🏻** - the "send-to-one-friend" CTA finger.
- **❤️** - "this content is offered to you with love" closing.
- **🤪 😉** - wink to signal a joke / soft mockery is coming.
- **❌ vs ✅** - used as a binary diptych inside slides (shark in small bowl ✅ vs small fish in big bowl ❌).
- **Asterisk self-censor** - "مخـ*درات" (drugs). Edge without ban.

## What to AVOID (anti-patterns that kill the voice)

- ❌ Generic motivation ("believe in yourself", "you've got this")
- ❌ Round numbers when authority matters ("$20K" instead of "$21,637.00")
- ❌ Multi-emoji clusters in body copy (🔥🔥💯💯) - looks like a junior influencer
- ❌ MSA on the gut line - kills the intimacy
- ❌ Khaliji on the framework line - kills the credibility
- ❌ More than ONE rhetorical question per slide
- ❌ Sourcing celebrities from his own region only - he uses GLOBAL icons (Bezos, Musk, Buffett, Jobs, Godin, Cardone, Ma) precisely because they're universally legible
- ❌ Hashtag stuffing > 9 tags
- ❌ Sales CTA before slide 7
- ❌ Highlighting EVERY phrase - only 1-3 strategic highlights per cover
- ❌ Closing with "what do you think?" generically. He closes with "send this to one person who needs it" - specific behavioral CTA.

## 9-point pre-publish checklist

Score 0-9. Anything <7 is a flop.

1. ☐ Cover headline names a SPECIFIC failure mode (not a generic goal)
2. ☐ Hook contains an external anchor (proper noun, specific $ amount, named brand, named famous person)
3. ☐ المبادر watermark on cover slide
4. ☐ Exactly one stretched-letter word (ـــــــ) somewhere on cover
5. ☐ Slides 2-5 introduce a NAMED framework with English acronym OR a 3-letter card grid (RACI / LTV / 4D / PBP / CAC)
6. ☐ Caption is < 4 lines and ends with a Khaliji question or apologetic preamble ("أعلم أن هذا لن يطرب بعض الناس...")
7. ☐ At least one verbatim signature phrase from the list above
8. ☐ Closing CTA is "send to one person ☝🏻" - not generic "what do you think?"
9. ☐ Code-switches MSA↔Khaliji at least once (frameworks in MSA, emotional punches in Khaliji)

## How to use this skill

**Trigger words:** "write like badr", "badrshaqer carousel", "khaliji business post", "mobader carousel", "arabic founder carousel", "tribute to badr".

**Workflow:**
1. Get the TOPIC from the user (e.g., "pricing", "hiring", "burnout").
2. Pick a hook archetype (1-8 above) that fits - prefer #1 (brand-callout) or #4 (myth-buster) for max-virality.
3. Cast a SINGLE celebrity/film still as the visual metaphor.
4. Draft the cover slide: 2-3 lines, one red highlight, one stretched letter, المبادر stamp.
5. Outline 10-19 inner slides around ONE named framework.
6. Write the caption: ≤ 4 lines, opens with a Khaliji question, ends without resolving.
7. Run the 9-point checklist. If <7, throw it out and re-hook.
